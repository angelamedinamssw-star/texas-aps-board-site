About the Board

APS Partners Board is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that collaborates with Adult Protective Services to strengthen community partnerships and improve outcomes for clients.

Mission

To support APS through community engagement, volunteer coordination, and resource development that center client safety, dignity, and equity.

Our Priorities

  • Engage community partners to better serve APS clients
  • Support consistent, values-driven practice
  • Provide clear information for the public and stakeholders
